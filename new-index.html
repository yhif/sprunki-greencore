About Sprunki GreenCore

Sprunki GreenCore is a fan-made mod of the beloved *Incredibox* music game, offering players an imaginative twist on music creation. This mod immerses users in a vibrant, nature-inspired world filled with lush visuals and soothing sounds. Players can mix beats, vocals, and melodies using a cast of animated characters, each contributing unique musical elements that reflect the serene theme of the game. Whether you're a seasoned musician or a casual player, Sprunki GreenCore invites everyone to enjoy the art of music-making in an enchanting environment.

How to Play

1. Select the Mod

Choose Sprunki GreenCore from the available options.

2. Load the Game

Click to enter the game and wait for it to load.

3. Choose Your Characters

Browse and select characters with unique sounds.

4. Drag and Drop Sounds

Drag characters onto the stage to create music.

5. Experiment with Combinations

Mix and match characters for new sounds.

6. Record Your Creation

Save your track using the record option.
