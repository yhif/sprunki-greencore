About Sprunki GreenCore

Overview

Sprunki GreenCore is a fan-made modification of Incredibox that reimagines the game with a focus on nature and sustainability. Through its distinctive green-inspired theme, players are immersed in a forest-like environment while creating music.

Gameplay

Using the familiar drag-and-drop gameplay, you can combine characters representing eco-friendly concepts like recycling, solar power, and wildlife conservation. Each character emits unique sounds that blend together to create an atmospheric soundtrack reminiscent of an enchanted forest.

Visual Design

The mod features vibrant visuals with lush greenery, flowing rivers, and other natural elements, creating a surreal yet earthy aesthetic that perfectly balances musical creativity with environmental themes.

Key Elements

  • Nature-inspired sound effects
  • Environmental character designs
  • Forest-themed animations
  • Eco-friendly visual elements

Key Features

Nature-Inspired Sounds

Experience organic sounds from rustling leaves to flowing water, creating a unique environmental symphony that transports you to a peaceful forest.

Eco-Themed Visuals

Immerse yourself in a vibrant green world with characters representing environmental themes, bringing nature's beauty to life.

Interactive Bonuses

Discover hidden combinations that unlock special animations and environmental effects, adding an extra layer of excitement to your music creation.

Dynamic Mixing

Create endless musical combinations with our intuitive mixing system, where every character interaction produces harmonious nature-inspired sounds.

How to Play

1

Select Your Beat

Start with a base rhythm by choosing from our nature-inspired beat characters. These form the foundation of your mix.

2

Add Melodies

Layer melodic elements by dragging forest-themed characters onto the stage. Each character adds unique nature sounds to your composition.

3

Mix & Match

Experiment with different combinations of characters. Try removing or adding elements to create your perfect environmental symphony.

4

Discover Bonuses

Find special combinations to unlock magical animations and bonus tracks. Each discovery adds new elements to your forest masterpiece.

Pro Tips

  • Start with rhythm characters to build a solid foundation
  • Mix different character types for richer compositions
  • Listen carefully to how sounds blend together

Controls

  • Click and drag characters to add them to your mix
  • Click on active characters to remove them
  • Use the record button to save your favorite mixes
